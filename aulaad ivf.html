About Aulaad IVF

Aulaad IVF & Fertility Center, established in 2024, builds upon more than a decade of clinical excellence in reproductive medicine. Led by Dr. Inam Ul Haq, the center delivers advanced fertility treatments with compassion, transparency, and ethical medical care.

Our mission is to make IVF treatment successful and accessible while ensuring emotional and medical support for every couple on their parenthood journey.

The clinic has received professional recognition including participation and awards at the Gujarat Medical Conference, reflecting our commitment to medical excellence.
